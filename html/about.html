Our Story

DiamondCut Motors was founded in 2005 by (-----), a car enthusiast with a vision to create the ultimate destination for luxury and exotic automobiles. With a small showroom in Beverly Hills, California, DiamondCut Motors aimed to offer discerning customers a curated selection of the finest cars in the world.

Within three years, DiamondCut Motors had established a reputation for excellence, attracting clients from all over the United States. The company expanded its showroom to accommodate a growing inventory of exclusive vehicles, including brands like Ferrari, Lamborghini, and Bentley.

By 2015, DiamondCut Motors had become a household name among luxury car aficionados. The company opened a second showroom in Miami, Florida, catering to the East Coast market. This expansion solidified DiamondCut Motors' position as a leading dealership for exotic cars in the United States.

Today, DiamondCut Motors continues to thrive, with a commitment to providing the finest luxury and exotic vehicles. The company is dedicated to staying at the forefront of the automotive industry, embracing new technologies and trends to offer unparalleled service and selection.
